About Word Embeddings

Word embeddings are powerful tools in Natural Language Processing that represent words as numerical vectors. These vectors capture the semantic meaning of words, allowing us to understand how words relate to each other.

This application allows you to explore these relationships by visualizing word embeddings and finding nearest neighbors to a given word.
